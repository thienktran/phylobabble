Request for Workshop Proposals on Clade Focused Tree-Data Integration Challenges sponsored by FuturePhy, OpenTree and Arbor. We invite 2 page proposals to fund small workshops and/or hackathons on completing the tree of life and integrating data layers for specific clades.
