Had the same problem, just clicked around forever until I figured it out. Click on trees -> Describe trees, and in the dialog, click the ‘states for internal nodes’ box. In the output window you may have to scroll up past the cladogram, if you didn’t untick the box for outputting it. It will give you a list of each node number together with its associated state.
